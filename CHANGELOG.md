## 0.1.1

* Default `trackWidth` is **110** (was 74 in early drafts).
* Added `CONTRIBUTING.md` and `SECURITY.md`.

## 0.1.0

* Initial release of `SmoothIOSToggle` with pill thumb (width = 2× height).
* Inline active/inactive labels beside the thumb (or inside the thumb).
* `SmoothToggleController` (`ValueNotifier`) and `SmoothToggleTheme` (`InheritedWidget`).
* Tap, drag, keyboard, semantics, and haptic feedback support.
* Works on Android, iOS, Web, Windows, macOS, and Linux.
